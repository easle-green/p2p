This privacy policy applies to all services and products provided by Polltopay. The following paragraphs explain how Polltopay use and handle your personal data. Privacy policy is presented in a more friendly and appealing way in order to help you quickly find the answers you really want about the privacy practices used by Polltopay.

Privacy Policy Highlights – Things that matter to you

For those creating surveys:

  • Your survey data belong to you and will never be seen, accessed, sold or delivered to anyone. Your survey data though can be used by the platform for better targeting and assisting the skipping logic of future surveys.
  • Your survey data are stored and kept securely at servers in the Russia.
  • Your survey data are processed and delivered to you at your wish.

For those answering surveys: 

  • Survey responses are collected anonymously.
  • Survey responses are not disclosed or sold to any third party. The survey creator controls your responses.
  • You will be prompted to provide your email only in case that you will be awarded. This information is kept securely, not disclosed to anyone and used only for informing you on how you will get your award.
  • Polltopay is the middleman responsible for delivering your responses to the survey creator. The survey creator is responsible for the content of the survey and the provided responses.

For those integrating Polltopay into their apps 

  • Only public information about you app is collected when you integrate Polltopay in your app.
  • Polltopay monitors and inform you about the survey responses gathered through your app.
  • Statistics and behavior of users on each survey served through your app are collected and stored to Polltopay servers.
